HealthLake Security & Compliance
===============================

Security and compliance are essential for any healthcare data platform. AWS HealthLake is designed to meet strict regulatory requirements and protect sensitive patient information.

Key Features:
- HIPAA-eligible and supports healthcare compliance standards
- Data encryption at rest and in transit
- Fine-grained access controls using AWS IAM
- Audit logging and monitoring with AWS CloudTrail
- Automated backup and disaster recovery options

Benefits:
- Ensures patient privacy and data protection
- Simplifies compliance for healthcare organizations
- Reduces risk of data breaches and unauthorized access
